# Welcome to Encompass Unlocked

You found it — a community built by an Encompass admin, for Encompass admins.

Whether you've been managing Encompass for years or you're just getting started 
with customizations, you're in the right place. This site is here to help you 
build things you didn't think were possible — without needing a developer on 
speed dial.

## The Mission

Most Encompass admins are in the same boat — you know exactly what your 
company needs, but writing JavaScript from scratch isn't in your job 
description. That's where Claude AI changes the game.

**Encompass Unlocked exists to show you that you can do this too.**

You don't need to be a developer. You need to know your business, know your 
Encompass environment, and be willing to try. This site gives you real examples 
you can learn from, adapt, and make your own.

---

## What You'll Find Here

- **Button click scripts** — Make your buttons actually do things. Stamp 
  fields, navigate to tools, trigger logic, show modals, and more.
- **Field logic** — Conditionally color fields, enforce rules, and control 
  what users can and can't do based on loan data.
- **Audit trails and field logging** — Track who did what and when, directly 
  inside the loan file.
- **Web code job aids** — Step-by-step guides with the full code, plain-English 
  explanations, and instructions for putting it to work in your environment.

Every page follows the same format: here's what it does, here's the code, 
here's how to use it. No fluff.
